## Features

**Zip Lines**  

Miramar now features ziplines to aid in quick player travel across hazardous or difficult to navigate areas of the map.

## Weather Events

**Dust Storm**  

Dust Storms appear randomly on the map. Entering the blizzard will reduce player visibility, and slowly reduce player health.
